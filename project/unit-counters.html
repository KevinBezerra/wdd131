The Unit Counter System

Success in Age of Empires II depends on understanding the "rock-paper-scissors" system. Nearly every unit has a counter, and building the right units to defeat your opponent's army is key to victory. Below are the most fundamental counters in the game.

Barracks Counter

Spearman-line

→

Cavalry

Spearmen and Pikemen are cost-effective infantry that deal massive bonus damage to cavalry. Always build them to defend against Knights and other mounted units.

Archery Range Counter

Skirmisher-line

→

Archers

Skirmishers have high pierce armor, making them resistant to arrow fire. They also deal bonus damage to archer-line units, making them the perfect counter.

Stable Counter

Light Cavalry

→

Monks & Siege

Fast-moving Light Cavalry and Hussars are excellent for raiding. They excel at quickly closing the distance to destroy slow-moving siege weapons and have high conversion resistance against monks.
